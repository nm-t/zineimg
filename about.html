what is this

hello everyone is a zine that you can fill in and also share online (if you want)!

draw some stuff on my template then upload it to share with your friends.

how do I do it

hopefully you already purchased a copy of hello everyone from me. if you have, follow the steps below. if you haven't, I dunno.

  1. fill out your zine!
  2. scan your completed zine
  3. rotate the scanned image so that the title page is in the top left corner, upside down
  4. upload your image
  5. share the link with your friends!
